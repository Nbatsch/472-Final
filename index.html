Interests

I have an incredibly wide variety of hobbies ranging from golfing to building computers. Once I find something I'm interested in I pour my heart into learning more about it; achieving a degree of proficiency in it, and sharing my love for that hobby with others.

Cooking

A humbling skill that gives me the opportunity to appreciate culture.

I’m passionate about exploring different cultures and learning about traditions that are different from my own. I believe food tells a lot about the land, identity, and struggles that people had to endure throughout history. Being someone with an immense appreciation for good food, I decided to learn how to cook to pay homage to the cultures that created such wonderful dishes, with the added bonus of being able to enjoy and share them with friends. I was fortunate to attend classes at the Culinary Institute of America at Copia to advance in my journey to create food that makes people happy.

Golf

A game that inspires personal growth.

Golf is the game that taught me patience, perseverance, and integrity. As a child I participated in and won several junior tournaments, but it wasn’t until high school when I really started loving the game; studying and practicing every day to sharpen my skills, understanding the physics behind the golf swing, and improving my strategy and “course management.” By junior year I became captain of the varsity team at Notre Dame Prep, leading my team to victory at regionals and scoring lowest for our team at states. I was on track to play college golf, but unfortunately herniated a disc at a Callaway tournament which ended my competitive golf career. After a great deal of physical therapy and acupuncture, I’m very blessed to be able to play the game that I love.
